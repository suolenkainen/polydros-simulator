## User Journey

### Step 1: View Inventory
Open Agent â†’ See all cards in list view with mini-graphs

### Step 2: Quick Scan
- See price trend at a glance in mini-graph
- Spot rising/falling cards instantly
- Check quality and desirability scores

### Step 3: Detailed View
- Click any card to expand
- See exact prices for last 10 ticks
- Grid format shows 2 columns for easy comparison

### Step 4: Close Details
- Click again to collapse
- Move to next card
- Or scroll to view more

### Before (Global Search)
- Search all cards globally
- Click to open modal

- Data in separate overlay

- Select agent
- Cards shown in list
- Click to expand inline

- Data stays in view
